Naturopathic Doctor

Mississauga & Markham Full-time / Part-time Join Our Talent Community

About the Opportunity

As a key member of our clinical team, you'll provide high-quality treatments that align with Revibe's mission of optimizing recovery, performance, and wellbeing. You'll play an active role in patient care, collaboration, and the continued growth of our wellness community.

Responsibilities

  • Deliver evidence-based naturopathic treatments tailored to individual needs
  • Conduct thorough assessments and develop personalized treatment plans
  • Maintain accurate and timely charting, notes, and treatment documentation in our internal system
  • Educate patients on post-treatment care, self-management, and healthy lifestyle practices
  • Communicate clearly and empathetically to guide patients through their care journey
  • Uphold Revibe's guest-first culture—ensuring each session is purposeful, personalized, and impactful
  • Collaborate closely with other internal clinicians to support holistic outcomes
  • Engage in mentorship programs, workshops, and internal education sessions
  • Participate in community initiatives, recovery events, and membership programs that promote Revibe's mission
  • Maintain treatment room cleanliness, organization, and equipment care according to clinic standards
  • Stay compliant with all regulatory requirements and maintain professional liability insurance
  • Support clinic performance through reliability, scheduling consistency, and patient retention

About You

  • Licensed and in good standing with your professional college
  • Passionate about holistic health, patient experience, and continuous learning
  • A team player who thrives in a collaborative, multidisciplinary setting
  • Professional, proactive, reliable, and eager to grow within a high-performing wellness environment
  • Committed to continuous growth and learning

Why You Should Join Revibe

  • Become a part of a collaborative, wellness-driven team culture
  • Access to a community-driven, state-of-the-art clinic environment
  • Opportunities for professional growth through mentorship, education, and leadership pathways
  • Competitive compensation structure with clear progression opportunities
  • Eligibility for extended health benefits
  • Flexible scheduling (full-time or part-time)
  • Full access to Revibe's recovery services (cold plunge, sauna, Normatec boots) and in-house gym facilities
