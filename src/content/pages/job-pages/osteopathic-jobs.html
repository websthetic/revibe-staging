We're Hiring

Osteopathic Manual Practitioner

Mississauga & Markham Full-time / Part-time Join Our Talent Community

About the Opportunity

As a key member of our clinical team, you'll provide high-quality treatments that align with Revibe's mission of optimizing recovery, performance, and wellbeing. You'll play an active role in patient care, collaboration, and the continued growth of our wellness community.

What You'll Do

  • Conduct comprehensive patient assessments, including medical history, posture, movement, and musculoskeletal evaluation
  • Develop individualized treatment plans based on osteopathic principles and patient needs
  • Deliver hands-on manual therapy including joint mobilization, soft tissue techniques, cranial or visceral approaches where appropriate
  • Educate patients on posture, ergonomics, exercises, and lifestyle strategies to support long-term health and recovery
  • Maintain accurate and timely clinical documentation in accordance with clinic standards and professional guidelines
  • Collaborate with other practitioners to provide coordinated, multidisciplinary care
  • Contribute to a welcoming and professional clinic environment for patients and team members

About You

  • Licensed member in good standing with your professional college
  • Experience in a multidisciplinary clinic is an asset
  • Strong knowledge of anatomy, biomechanics, and musculoskeletal health
  • Excellent manual therapy and assessment skills
  • Strong communication and patient education abilities
  • Professional, proactive, reliable, and eager to grow within a high-performing wellness environment
  • Committed to continuous growth and learning

Why You Should Join Revibe

  • Become a part of a collaborative, wellness-driven team culture
  • Access to a community-driven, state-of-the-art clinic environment
  • Opportunities for professional growth through mentorship, education, and leadership pathways
  • Competitive compensation structure with clear progression opportunities
  • Eligibility for extended health benefits
  • Flexible scheduling (full-time or part-time)
  • Full access to Revibe's recovery services (cold plunge, sauna, Normatec boots) and in-house gym facilities
