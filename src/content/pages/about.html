June 2019

Revibe Opens Its Doors

Revibe officially opened its first location with a simple idea: create a place where people could move better, recover properly, and feel supported. What started as a vision between two young entrepreneurs quickly became a space where community and care naturally came together. From day one, the goal was bigger than just treatment. It was about helping people feel capable again.

January 2020

The World Changes

Only a few months after opening, COVID hit and the world slowed down. Like many small businesses, we had to adapt quickly and navigate uncertainty. It was a challenging time, but it strengthened our belief in the importance of health, movement, and community. The experience made us more resilient and more focused on our mission.

2022 – 2023

A Hard Lesson Downtown

We opened a downtown location with big hopes for expanding the Revibe community. Ultimately, it didn't work out the way we planned. It was a tough chapter, but an important one. The lessons we learned there shaped how we think about growth today.

October 2023

A New Believer Joins the Mission

Former NBA player Andrew Nicholson joined Revibe as an investor. Having someone who understands performance and recovery at the highest level believe in the vision meant a lot to our team. His support helped reinforce what we were building. It gave us confidence to keep pushing forward.

2024 – Present

Growing the Vision

Revibe expanded with the opening of our Markham location. Since then, the business has continued to reach new financial and community milestones. Each step forward has helped strengthen the foundation we started building in 2019. And we remain focused on delivering athlete-grade care, reimagined for everyBody.
